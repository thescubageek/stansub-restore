The Location

Roatan is located on the edge of the Cayman Trench. This feature is 25,200 feet deep at its center (the Grand Canyon is less than 6,000 feet deep). The first one thousand to two thousand feet of this happens in the form of a vertical wall, literally a stone's throw from Roatan's shore in places. This fact makes the entire operation possible. If the deep water were not so close to shore, the submersible would need a surface support ship to transport it, and the operating costs would be prohibitive.

Access to deep water is only one requirement, however. One must also be located near a protected harbor and a sizable number of tourists. Hence, only two deep diving submersibles for tourists operate worldwide.

We launch at Half Moon Bay in West End, Roatan Honduras, from the dock which we share with Half Moon Bay Cabins, an excellent place to stay when visiting Roatan. Roatan OnLine is a guide to Roatan, Honduras with hotels, diving, pictures, and cultural information for visitors to Roatan.
