Statistics on C-BUG

  • Length, 12 feet Width 5 feet 6 inches

  • Payload   approximately 320 lbs.

  • Hull 1/4 inch thick steel with 7 reinforcing ribs that make the hull 1 inch thick every 18 inches

  • View ports- 8 for the passenger and 9 for the pilot constructed of inch and 1/4 thick Plexiglas

  • Max depth achieved to date  - 725 feet

  • Design depth  - 700 feet

  • Current Operational depth - 500 feet

  • Life support- three days supply- O2 carried inside as well as CO2 absorbent Safety features.

  • 6 independent ballast tanks, any one of which is capable of bringing the sub to the surface

  • Twin redundant high pressure air systems two drop weights  (each 170 lbs.)

  • Self righting in case of capsize

  • Daytime and night time flares for emergency surface locating
