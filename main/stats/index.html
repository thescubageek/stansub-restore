Statistics

  • Length, 12 feet
  • Width 5 feet 6 inches
  • Payload   apx 800 lbs
  • Hull 1/4 inch thick steel with 7 reinforcing ribs that make the hull 1 inch thick every 18 inches
  • Viewports- 8 4 for the passenger and 4 for the pilot  constructed of inch and 1/4 thick plexiglass
  • Max depth achieved to date  - 725 feet
  • Design depth  - 700 feet
  • Life support- three days supply- O2 carried inside as well as CO2 absorbant
  • Safety features.
  • - 6 independent ballast tanks, any one of which is capable of bringing the sub to the surface
  • -twin redundant high pressure air systems
  • -two drop weights  (each 170 lbs.)
  • - self righting in case of capsize
  • - daytime and night time flares for emergency surface locating
