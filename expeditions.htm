The Expedition Options

There are 3 primary dive options.
Note: All pricing is PER TRIP (per person pricing is half):

1000ft - 1 1/2 Hours - $600.00 USD

We explore the second wall which drops between 200- 700 feet, and the base of it where huge bolders have fallen. At 1000 ft we can often see over 200 feet horizontally, with only natural light. Animals encountered include sea lillies, glass sponges, pom-pom anenomes, and lace coral.




1500ft - 2 1/2 Hours - $800.00 USD

We spend an entire extra hour picking along the boulder fields at the base of the wall. Cat sharks and two types of lobster are commonly spotted. Doing this dive in the evening increases the chance of see more bizarre creatures, coming from deeper water to feed.

2000ft - 3 1/2 Hours - $1000.00 USD

We have finally made it to the land of perpetual darkness. This region of the ocean covers more than half the planet, and have never known daylight, nor seasons. Many of the creatures date back before the dinosaurs. Fish encountered include jelly-nosed eels, rough sharks, and chimera.


Deciding between day or night

These above dives can be scheduled any time of day or night. Due to the water becoming crystal clear at 300 feet, spectacular views can be enjoyed looking up the wall in the daytime hours. At night however, the submarine will encounter schools of squid, bristle mouth fish, and hatchet fish, that would never be seen during the day.

6 Gilled Shark Dive

1500+ft - 4 - 8 Hours - $1500.00 USD

For this special adventure, we leave after dinner and with bait attached to the front of the sub. We find a suitable ledge below 1500 feet and lay in wait for the enormous 6-gilled sharks to apear. These sharks are one of the most prehistoric shark species, and can grow to 20ft. A shark sighting is guaranteed, if not we go again. Waits can be up to 3 hours.
