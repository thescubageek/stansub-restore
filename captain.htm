The Captain

Captain Karl Stanley has spent over two-thirds of his life pursuing his desire to explore the planet's "final frontier". He has designed and built two deep diving submersibles which he has piloted in three different countries on more than 700 dives, with missions ranging from just a few hours up to seventeen hours. Stanley has a degree in American Studies from Eckerd College. At the age of 24, he began taking people for trips to see for themselves the deep waters of Roatan. He has been featured in stories by National Geographic* and on the Discovery Channel.

Karl Stanley is a member of the Deep Submersible Pilots Association and the ACLU; he is available for technnical consulting and public speaking.

*National Geographic Adventure, November 2002, page 92; interview on National Geographic Adventure web site.
